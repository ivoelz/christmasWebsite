Christmas Facts

Did you know?

Santa Claus didn't always dress up in red clothes.

Santa Claus originally wore colors like purple, green, or blue. The reason Santa Claus now wears red clothing is because Coca Cola dressed him in red to match their brand and it stuck!


Did you know?

Christmas trees are an ancient tradition.

The tradition of Christmas trees goes back to ancient Romans and Egyptians. They marked trees as a reminder that spring would return.


Did you know?

The song "Jingle Bells" was originally a Thanksgiving song.

James Lord Pierpont wrote "One Horse Open Sleigh" for a Thanksgiving church concert. The song was renamed and is now one of the most popular Christmas songs.
